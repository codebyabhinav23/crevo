About Crevo

Crevo is a community-driven platform built for creators, developers, and designers to showcase their work and receive constructive feedback. Our mission is to provide a space where you can share your projects, get valuable insights, and connect with peers.

Whether you're a student looking for feedback on a class project, a hobbyist wanting to share your latest creation, or a developer building a tool, Crevo is the place to get eyes on your work. Upvotes help the best projects rise to the top, and our upcoming review features will help you refine your skills.

FAQs

Project scores are primarily based on community upvotes. Each upvote increments the project's score. We may introduce more complex scoring based on review quality in the future.

You can submit any personal project, hackathon entry, or open-source tool. We welcome everything from web apps and mobile apps to data science models and UI/UX designs.

Currently, we operate on a community-trust basis. We are exploring verification options for future updates to enhance platform integrity.

We encourage constructive feedback. While there is no downvote button, we plan to introduce a detailed review and comment section where you can provide helpful feedback. This is coming soon!
